Who We Are

A Voice for Accountability & Honest Inquiry

Pursuit Truth is a not-for-profit group which aims to pursue the truth. We operate as an independent platform free from commercial or political influence, committed to evidence-based analysis and open dialogue.

Our work spans corporate accountability, environmental integrity, and philosophical inquiry — challenging narratives that obscure reality and bringing rigorous scrutiny to claims made in public, academic, and corporate spheres.

We believe that access to the truth is a public good, and we make our research freely available to all who seek it.

What We Do

Our Areas of Focus

We pursue truth across three interconnected domains that shape how societies understand themselves and the world around them.

01

Corporate Accountability

Scrutinising corporate claims against measurable outcomes. We examine the gap between stated commitments and actual business practices, particularly in sustainability and ESG reporting.

02

Environmental Integrity

Investigating greenwashing — deceptive sustainability claims — and their real-world impact on markets, regulation, and public trust. We apply quantitative indices to measure corporate authenticity.

03

Philosophical Inquiry

Exploring timeless frameworks for understanding truth, reasoning, and knowledge. From classical logic to Eastern philosophical traditions, we situate modern questions within enduring intellectual contexts.

Greenwashing ESG Corporate

Does Greenwashing Pay Off? A Profit Paradox in Large Firms

Using a Greenwashing Index — defined as the gap between ESG scores and media controversies — our research reveals that deceptive sustainability claims can actually correlate with higher profitability in large firms, posing a systemic challenge to market-based accountability mechanisms.
